🚴🏼‍♀️ Interactive Bike Map

This is an interactive map that visualizes the bike paths in Boston and Cambridge, Massachusetts as indicated by the blue lines. The circles represent individual BlueBike stations. The size of each circle represents the amount of traffic at each station, while the color represents whether most traffic is entering or leaving the station. You can pan and zoom just like in Google Maps. Also, there is a slider at the top to allow you to filter the traffic by the time of day!
